What we do

We provide horticultural services for both public and private organizations. These workshops and events promote the use of the creative art of Flower Arranging in which participants learn how to arrange fresh flowers mixed with silk flowers to create a centerpiece. Arranging flowers provides the individual participants the opportunity to express themselves creatively and independently making floral arranging activities ideal in senior centers, nursing homes, adult daycare facilities, afterschool programs, fundraiser events and as an entertaining party idea. In addition to it's versatility, floral arranging offers the benefits of Horticultural Therapy, which promotes mental wellness, in that it has a positive effect on an individual's mood.

Our Workshops

Lord's Floral Creations was founded in 1987 as a retail flower shop, and proceeded to venture off to serve the senior population and afterschool programs, by instructing basic flower design. These lessons mainly focus on implementing the flower arraging pattern of the cross and, through our Garden of Eden Project, teach how seeds reproduce after their own kind when planted in good soil, representing how our actions become seeds of Good or Bad, that we plant in our lives, becoming our future Harvest.
These workshops are accompanied with music and lesson plans that follow a seasonal theme.
