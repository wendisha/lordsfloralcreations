Our Workshops

Lord's Floral Creations was founded in 1987 as a retail flower shop, and proceeded to venture off to serve the senior population, as well as afterschool programs, by instructing basic flower design. These lessons mainly focus in implementing the pattern of cross and teaching how planted seeds reproduce after their own kind. These workshops are accompanied with music and lesson plans that follow a seasonal theme.
